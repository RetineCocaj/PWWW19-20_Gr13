ALUMNI Community

Welcome to your alumni community of more than 300,000 graduates. Your relationship with us continues long after your graduation day so make sure you keep checking back for the latest news, event invites and opportunities to stay connected to Birmingham and each other.

You can access a range of benefits and services as an alumnus of our University. This includes alumni of the independent member institutions, the School of Advanced Study and the University of London Institute in Paris. It helps you stay connected with the University through regional clubs, affinity groups, and volunteer networks.

Need to get a foot in the door for your next job, or curious if an organization that’s pursuing you is the right fit? Use your online alumni directory to connect with people who could be great resources for informational interviews or introductions.

You can apply for free membership of the University Library, enabling you to borrow books on campus and have access to a large number of e-journal articles via the library walk-in access service. All you need to do is provide proof of study or graduation.


ALUMNI Experiences

Your experiences, expertise and knowledge are valuable! There are many ways you can give back – and when you do, you’ll be helping to guarantee the future excellence of the school and its students. There are many ways to do this, because contributing to personal development – as well as skills and knowledge – can help students, academics and people in business to be a force for positive change, as critical, creative, caring and collaborative thinkers and doers. Alumni can act as mentors, guest lecturers, panel members in student competitions, interviewers for prospective candidates, or you can give current students the benefit of your experience with career guidance.
